Opera House

Starting off with the most iconic landmark in Sydney, the opera house! There are multiple ways to experience the Sydney Opera House. Not all experiences need to cost money, the reason the building is so famous is because of its unique architecture and location. The best way to admire the building is to walk around the area and simply view the architecture. If you want to go inside the building, there are tours available for about 50$ for an adult, and if you want to see a show it will cost you anywhere from 70$ to several hundred dollars depending on both the show and the seating.

Harbour Bridge Climb

Take the climb on the Sydney Harbour Bridge to see the view and feel the fresh air. With multiple choices of time and day to do the climb, and how long of a climb you want to do, this is a must do when in Sydney if you aren't afraid of heights. With prices ranging from about 168$ to 400$ depending on what kind of climb you want to do and when. Although the price is high, the experience is worth the hole in your wallet.

Bondi Beach Surfing

Visit the world-famous Bondi Beach, which is accessible by a short bus trip from Sydney's city center costing 1-4$. Surfing lessons are available for all ages and skill levels. Although most water based activities are exhausting, there are many great places to dine close to the beach. Speaking from experience, Chuck Trailer's was a great place to grab a bite, in true Aussie fashion the people were some of the most pleasant.

Whale Safari

Go on a whale safari from Sydney to see the majestic humpback whales that migrate along the coast. The best time to see whales is from May to November, when they are migrating north to breed and give birth in the warmer waters of Queensland. Some tours even offer the chance to swim with dolphins or snorkel in the ocean. Overall, a whale safari from Sydney is a unique and unforgettable experience that allows you to witness these magnificent creatures in their natural habitat. Several companies offer whale watching tours from Sydney, with prices ranging from around $50 to $150 per person depending on the duration and type of tour.

Royal Botanic Garden

The Royal Botanic Garden is a large, beautiful garden located in Sydney that contains a habitat for various different plants and animals. It is a great place to go for a walk, relax, and see some of the beautiful flora and fauna that Australia has to offer. This experience could be great after any hectic day, as tiring as vacation on the other side of the world is. To walk through the garden is completely free, although events and guided tours may have a fee.
